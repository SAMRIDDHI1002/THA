Lucknow

Lucknow is the capital city of Indian state, Uttar Pradesh.
It spans across the vast expanse of 402 km2 631 km2.


Lucknow is known for embroidery works including chikankari, zari, zardozi, kamdani chikankari and gota making (gold lace weaving).
Chikankari is an embroidery work well known all over India. Chikankari constitutes 'shadow work' and is a delicate and artistic hand embroidery.
